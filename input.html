Outline: How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly or jam
  • Bread slices

Instructions:

  1. Take two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spread jelly or jam on the other slice.
  4. Press the slices together to form a sandwich.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half (optional).

Note: You can also add additional ingredients like honey or banana slices for variation.

Enjoy your delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich!
